Use the client's exact words for all slide and design copy. Never rewrite, rephrase, or add content that wasn't provided.

**Why:** The client knows their message. Rewriting introduces words they didn't choose and didn't approve — this is their brand voice, not Nagi's.

**How to apply:**
- Copy goes directly from the brief or the client's message onto the design — verbatim
- If content is missing for a section, flag it and ask — don't fill in
- Images are never stretched or distorted — always maintain aspect ratio (object-fit: contain or cover as appropriate, never transform: scaleX/scaleY unevenly)
