On every Instagram profile assessment, the agent must open the three-dot menu → "About this account" → read "Account based in" before accepting any country claim. This is the primary signal — it takes precedence over flag emojis, city mentions, caption language, and any other indicator.

**Why:** US KOL candidates turned out to be Philippines-based accounts. The team was relying on bio text and content signals instead of checking the registered country. Bio signals are unreliable — creators can list a US city while being registered elsewhere.

**How to apply:** C2 (country verification) starts with "About this account" on every Instagram profile, before any other demographic assessment. A mismatch between the target market and the registered country = fail C2, skip immediately. This step is not skippable even when bio and language signals strongly suggest a match.
