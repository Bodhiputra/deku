Write each KOL to Notion the moment it is confirmed (C2 passed, content fit passed, follower range passed). Do not accumulate a list and batch-write at the end of the session.

**Why:** User explicitly instructed this. Batching at the end loses the incremental checkpoint benefit and is the wrong workflow order — find 1, write 1, find next.

**How to apply:** After every KOL passes all qualification gates, immediately create the Notion record before moving on to discover the next candidate. This applies to all KOL research sessions regardless of target count.
